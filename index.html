How to Activate Your TV

  1. Power on your TV using the remote or power button.
  2. Select “English” or your preferred language.
  3. Set region as United States (USA).
  4. Connect to a secure Wi-Fi network.
  5. Accept Terms & Conditions.
  6. Install any available system updates.
  7. Note the activation code displayed.
  8. Visit www.tvsetup.com/activate on another device.
  9. Enter the activation code.
  10. Complete the steps shown to finish setup.
  11. Sign in with your provider or create a new account.
  12. Customize your preferences and enjoy!
