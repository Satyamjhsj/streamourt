TV Activation Instructions

Get your TV up and running in minutes with StreamOurTV

Activation Process

  1. Turn on your TV and ensure it's connected to Wi-Fi.
  2. Open the activation screen to get your unique code.
  3. On your smartphone or computer, go to the official activation website.
  4. Enter the code exactly as shown on your TV screen.
  5. Sign in or create an account to complete the activation.
  6. Once done, your TV will refresh and display the home screen.
  7. Test by opening any app to confirm successful activation.

Troubleshooting

  • No Internet? Restart your router and reconnect.
  • Invalid Code? Request a new code on your TV.
  • Website not loading? Clear your browser cache or switch browsers.
  • Still stuck? Reach out to our support team below.
